What is the Course and Faculty Management System?

The Course and Faculty Management System is a platform designed to streamline the management of academic courses and faculty information, facilitating easier scheduling, record-keeping, and communication.

Can I upload course materials?

Yes, faculty members can upload course materials such as lecture notes, assignments, and readings through the course management interface.

Is my data secure?

Yes, the system employs robust security measures, including encryption and regular backups, to ensure that user data is secure.

Who do I contact for technical support?

For technical support, users can contact the IT support team through the 'Help' section of the system or via email/phone provided on the support page.
